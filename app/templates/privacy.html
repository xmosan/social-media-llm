Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Sabeel Studio respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered Islamic content creation platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information from and about users of our platform, including:

  • Account Information: Personal identifiers such as your name, email address, and account preferences when you register.
  • Connected Social Media Accounts: Access tokens and profile information for social media platforms (such as Instagram via Meta APIs) that you choose to connect.
  • Content Data: The text, prompts, and visual assets you generate, draft, or schedule within Sabeel Studio.

3. How We Use Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve our services, specifically:

  • To facilitate the generation and scheduling of content to your connected accounts.
  • To refine and improve the accuracy of our AI-generated outputs.
  • To maintain the technical connection to your social media platforms.

4. Third-Party Services

Sabeel Studio integrates with several trusted third-party providers to deliver our core features:

  • Meta (Instagram/Facebook APIs): To publish and manage content on your behalf.
  • AI Providers: To process prompts and generate intelligent Islamic content.
  • Islamic Content APIs: To source verified Quran and Hadith data.

5. Data Storage & Protection

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security protocols. Sabeel Studio does not sell your personal data to third parties. We treat your content and connection data with the highest level of confidentiality.

6. User Control

You retain full control over your data. At any time, you may:

  • Disconnect your social media accounts from the settings panel.
  • Request the deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Modify or delete generated content and drafts.
